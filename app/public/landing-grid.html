The Problem

Goals fail when they skip the self

You've tried habit trackers. Morning routines. The 21-day challenges. They work for a while, then dissolve. Not because you lack discipline, but because you started in the wrong place.

Real change doesn't begin with goals. It begins with understanding who you already are: your energy patterns, your hidden motivations, the unconscious behaviors that shape your days.

"The curious paradox is that when I accept myself just as I am, then I can change."
— Carl Rogers
